What on Earth Happened to Patrick?

If you ever have the pleasure of capturing an adult Red-tailed Hawk you'll notice first how mesmerizing and penetrating her eyes are.

You'll next realize that the hypnosis isn't just because of her direct eye contact and the amber hue.

It's because though the hand with which you hold her feet is nervously fidgeting, her body moving around with it, her eyes, and indeed her entire head, are perfectly still.

I think of my life that way... wavering and frantic, a roller coaster, really. Somehow, I'm not affected.


Ugh. No sense in trying to be deep. Let's just get to it.

Where I've Been

We moved here from Augusta, Georgia. And I moved there from Cleveland. And I moved there from a string of other places after I was born in Connecticut about an hour from Manhattan.

The plan was to finish grad school here in Denver, then move back to the East Coast. But as it turns out, Colorado is a great place to stay and Georgia is a fantastic place to never return.

What I've Done

I think of my life as charmed. I've been a musician who's performed on some of Nashville's and New Orleans' biggest stages. I've been a Golf Pro who's played the Masters Tees at the Augusta National. I've climbed the Great Wall of China and eaten Halo-Halo on the streets of Manilla. Sipped coffee next to heavily armed guards in Jamaica. Worse, I paid $12 for an Earl Grey tee bag in Seoul.

I've been to a lot of hospitals to welcome life and say goodbye.

Had a week long stint or two in modelling. I don't recommend it. I've been a teacher and an analyst. A freelance web developer and a restaurant server. I've sold jeans and shipping containers full of wine.

I don't drink or smoke but from time to time I've been known to keep up with the Kardashians... That Khloe just understands me.

My first time abroad my passport was stolen. I am a cautionary tale to any students of Daniels College of Business embarking on international travel.
